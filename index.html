Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in your web design packages?

Our comprehensive packages include custom design, responsive development, SEO optimization, hosting, SSL certificate, and 12 months of maintenance and support. We also provide training for your team to manage content updates.

How long does it take to complete a website project?

Most projects are completed within 4-6 weeks. Complex e-commerce sites may take 8-10 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation and keep you updated throughout the process.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance and support?

Yes, all our packages include 12 months of maintenance, security updates, and technical support. After the first year, you can choose from our flexible maintenance plans starting at $99/month.

What makes your websites SEO-friendly?

We implement comprehensive SEO best practices including optimized code structure, fast loading speeds, mobile responsiveness, schema markup, and content optimization. Our sites consistently achieve higher search rankings.

What are your payment terms?

We offer flexible payment plans with 50% due at project start and the remainder upon completion. Monthly payment plans are available for larger projects. We accept all major credit cards and bank transfers.
